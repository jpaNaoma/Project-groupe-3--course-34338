\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Monitors a photocell sensor and a tilt switch to detect potential tampering or break-\/ins. 

This program reads data from a photocell sensor and a tilt switch. Based on the readings, it detects suspicious activities like tampering or a forceful break-\/in and sends alerts.
